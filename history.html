History

HISTORY

Olato represents a shift in the venture capital paradigm. We don't lead with wallets; we lead with craft. Our history is built on the belief that a startup's digital foundation is its strongest asset.

Starting as a focused design collective, we saw that visionary founders often struggled most in the initial phase: translating a breakthrough idea into a world-class digital presence. Olato was founded to provide that missing link, offering elite design systems, branding guidelines, and high-conversion landing pages in exchange for a true partnership.

Today, we operate a global network that bridges the gap between pre-seed concepts and tier-1 funding rounds. With over 300 VC connections in India and 40+ internationally, we ensure that our partner startups are not just designed to win, but connected to scale.

We are entrepreneur-first, investing our expertise and our network into the founders who are shaping the digital economy.

Our core team is based in India, supporting bold ventures across the globe.
