The Digital Logo represents Draper’s forward-facing identity. Built for the founders, innovators, and industries shaping the next century. Its bold geometry and modern type reflect a commitment to progress, technology, and global ambition. Designed to lead in digital environments, from the website and social platforms to founder-facing events, this mark signals Draper’s role at the forefront of the future.
